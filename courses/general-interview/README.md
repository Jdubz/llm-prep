# General Interview Techniques

Interview strategy, behavioral questions, and common technical topics for senior/staff-level engineering roles. Covers the non-coding dimensions that often determine the outcome at senior levels — communication, leadership narratives, and asking the right questions.

> **Perspective**: At 10+ years experience, interviews shift from "can you code?" to "can you lead, communicate, and make good decisions?" This course covers the meta-skills that complement your technical courses.
